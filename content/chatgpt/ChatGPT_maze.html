Give me a recipe to make acai ice cream at home with no ice cream maker

To make acai ice cream at home with no ice cream maker, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen acai berries
  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries (such as strawberries, raspberries, and blueberries)
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup coconut cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped almonds

Here is a step-by-step recipe to make acai ice cream at home with no ice cream maker:

  1. Place the frozen acai berries and mixed berries in a food processor, and blend until smooth.

  2. Add the coconut milk, maple syrup, and coconut cream to the food processor, and blend until well combined.

  3. Transfer the mixture to a shallow container, and place it in the freezer.

  4. After 30 minutes, remove the container from the freezer, and use a fork to stir the mixture and break up any ice crystals that have formed.

  5. Place the container back in the freezer, and repeat this process every 30 minutes for 2-3 hours, or until the mixture is frozen and scoopable.

  6. Serve the acai ice cream in bowls, and top with chopped almonds.

Enjoy your homemade acai ice cream!
