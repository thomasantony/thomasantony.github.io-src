Write a scene from House M.D about the early days of the Covid-19 pandemic

It was early March, and the Covid-19 pandemic was just beginning to take hold in the United States. Dr. House and his team were in the middle of a routine day at the hospital when they received word that a patient in the ICU had tested positive for the virus.

House, ever the skeptic, was initially dismissive of the news, but as more and more cases began to surface, he realized the gravity of the situation. The hospital was quickly overwhelmed, and House and his team were forced to make difficult decisions about who to treat and who to prioritize.

As the virus continued to spread, House found himself on the front lines, fighting to save the lives of his patients while also struggling to protect himself and his team from infection. He worked long hours, often forgoing sleep and proper meals, but despite his best efforts, some patients still succumbed to the virus.

It was a trying time for House and his team, but they persevered and continued to do their best to help those in need. As the months went on and a vaccine was developed, the situation slowly began to improve, but the memory of those early, difficult days of the pandemic would stay with them forever.

INT. HOSPITAL ROOM - DAY
